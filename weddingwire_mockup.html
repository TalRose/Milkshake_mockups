About

$1,549 Most Popular Price

A full-service wedding DJ in Gainesville, Virginia, Bobby G's Disc Jockeys is plays just the right mix of music to entertain you and your guests during your Special day. Veteran Owned and operated by Robert Goeddel, the company has been an integral part of the Virginia wedding industry since 1994. Working closely with each couple to plan the perfect affair, Bobby G's Disc Jockeys is the ideal choice for keeping your guests on the dance floor all night long!

Services Offered
Bobby G's Disc Jockeys takes prides in the professionalism presented at each Ceremony and Reception. For a one-of-a-kind entertainment experience, the following services are provided:

  • Consultation
  • Sound/PA system (extra system avail for Ceremony)
  • Emcee services
  • Up Lights
  • Photo Booth
  • Karaoke
  • Setup (we do not charge for setup of equipment)
  • Breakdown (we do not charge for breakdown of equipment)
  • Liability Insurance (we are fully insured)
  • Military Discount for prior service, current service or retired.
  • Discount given to Police and Fire Departments. Current or retired


Areas Covered
Bobby G's Disc Jockeys happily serves couples located throughout Northern Virginia. In addition, services are extended to those in Maryland and Washington, D.C. We have traveled to Southern Virginia, New Jersey and Pennsylvania for special events.

    Bobby G was the PERFECT MC/DJ for our wedding

    Bobby G was so warm and professional throughout the planning and many changes for our 2020 wedding. On the big day he arrived early and brought all the equipment which he moved from our outdoor ceremony to the indoor reception. We originally planned to have the cocktail hour at a 2nd outdoor location and he agreed to that move as well - he just could not have been more accommodating. He acted like an event director and kept us on time without ever feeling rushed. He made several suggestions that added so much to the day - like a special dance for married couples where at intervals he asked couples married less than 5 years to sit down, less than 10 years, etc - leaving the 1 or 2 couples married the longest still standing at the end of the song, then asked them for their marriage advice. He even made us a CD of key songs from our ceremony, cocktail hour and reception! He read the mood of the room incredibly well - there were a few songs I had requested for the cocktail hour and reception that totally killed the vibe and he quickly faded them out and put on a different song that brought the life back to the party.
    Do yourself a favor and hire this man for your wedding or special event - you will not regret it!
