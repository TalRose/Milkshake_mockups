The Most Iconic Rock n’ Roll Groupies Of All Time

by Archie Clarke
June 12, 2021

Every artist needs his muse to bring out the best emotions and create masterpieces. Many of the musicians that we love to listen to have always found their muse in some girl that they hang out with or are involved with in a love affair. These women are often known as groupies and they have been responsible for some of the greatest hits over the years. Apart from being the muses of the singers and songwriters, they have also captured the hearts of many of these famous artists and each one is special in their own way. Read on to find out the reason why some of these groupies have been the source of inspiration for many rock n’roll stars.

Lori Mattix

Also known as Lori Lightning, Mattix was a “baby” groupie of the 1970s rock era, starting her groupie lifestyle at 14 years old. She spent time with David Bowie, Iggy Pop, Jimmy Page, and Mick Jagger.
