Golden Time Signal : E21 - 07/14/2021

Synopsis

Your illness is not a sudden threat: your body kept sending signals to warn you. Golden Time Signal, a new healthcare/information show, focuses on those preceding signs from the body and informs how to prevent the illness before it happens. The program aims to raise awareness of our physical health by studying various cases, including celebrities who overcame health problems.
