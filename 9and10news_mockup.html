Florida Apartment Collapse Kills At Least 1, Police On Scene

June 24, 2021
9and10news Site Staff,
  • COLLAPSE 2
    Show Caption Hide Caption
  • COLLAPSE 3
    Show Caption Hide Caption
  • COLLAPSE 1
    Show Caption Hide Caption

UPDATE 6:10 a.m. – Police confirm at least one person has died.

Emergency crews are on the scene after an apartment building partially collapsed in near Miami Beach early Thursday morning.

One side of the Surfside, Florida complex is completely destroyed, with rubble covering the ground.

Surfside police say multiple agencies are at the scene.

They say it’s not yet clear how many people were inside, but the building has 136 units.

One witness says the collapse felt and sounded like an earthquake or tornado.

9&10 News is working to get the latest details on this developing story.
